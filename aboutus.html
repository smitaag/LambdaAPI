Our Story

"We are changing the world with technology" - said Bill Gates.

The founding team at BiGPiQ has always enjoyed the association with Technology.
But our commitment to technology intensified and our belief in it became stronger than ever when we realized how it can help society when the pandemic hit us in early 2020.


Rightly said by Anthony Robbins - "Every problem is a gift - without problems, we would not grow".

The inspiration took us to participate in the CoWIN grand challenge launched by the Indian Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW) and the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) for building the digitalized platform to be used to effectively roll out and scale up the mechanism for Covid Vaccine Distribution System at the national level.


A quote from Arthur C. Clarke - "Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic!".

That was it! When we decided on making the move ourselves and creating magic with Technology.
